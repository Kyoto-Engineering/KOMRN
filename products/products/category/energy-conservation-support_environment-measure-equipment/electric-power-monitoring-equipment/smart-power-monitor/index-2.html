Smart Power Monitor

These Components monitor the power used by each machine in the factory to detailed energy management to support countermeasures to save power.

Smart Power Monitor List

There are 5 products of Smart Power Monitor.

Power Monitor KM-N3-FLK

Global Power Monitor for On-panel Installation

Power Monitor KM-N2-FLK

Global Power Monitor for Mounting Inside Control Panels

Multi-circuit Smart Power Monitor KM1

New Ways to Uncover Power Savings. Measure Multiple Distribution Panels at the Same Time.

Smart Power Monitor KM50-E

Power and current can be measured simultaneously. Measurement of generated power (regenerative power), leading reactive power, lagging reactive power, and consumed power. High-performance Power Monitor.

Smart Power Monitor KM50-C

Power and current can be measured simultaneously. Measurement of generated power (regenerative power), leading reactive power, lagging reactive power, and consumed power. Low Cost Power Monitor.
