Detects Broken Drills in Environments with Sprayed Oil Mist

Case Number 49

last update: November 4, 2009

This application uses a Laser Sensor with a Built-in Amplifier and a long detection distance.
This Laser Photoelectric Sensor has a long detection distance and is ideal for drills.

Know-How

Conventional photoelectric sensors with built-in amplifiers and a large spot diameter could not detect small diameter drills.

Benefit / Efficiency

The Laser Photoelectric Sensor with a small spot light and long detection distance that is used at high power enables accurate long distance detection without being affected by oil mist.
