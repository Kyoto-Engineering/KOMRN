Detection of Discharged Powder Quantitie

Case Number 296

last update: July 7, 2009

This application measures and monitors quantities of discharged powder.

An Analog Photoelectric Sensor detects the quantity of discharged powder. A Process Indicator scales and displays the linear output of 4 to 20 mA. Also, the system sends BCD data to the PLC to monitor the quantity of discharge.

Know-How

The system uses a Linear Proximity Sensor and a K3HB-X Process Indicator, which are ideal to measure and judge analog quantities.

Benefit / Efficiency

The system automatically monitors the quantity of discharged powder.
