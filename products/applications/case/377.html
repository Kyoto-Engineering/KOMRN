Detects Aluminum Caps

Case Number 377

last update: April 13, 2015

You can use a special proximity sensor for aluminum to detect any missing caps.

Know-How

This compact sensor with non-ferrous detecting can easily detect aluminum caps and can be installed while you are checking the sensing excess gain level at a glance on the level indicators.

Benefit / Efficiency

The simple teaching function to easily adjust sensitivity prevents differences in adjustment and the self-diagnosis function provides remarkable performance for the maintenace.
