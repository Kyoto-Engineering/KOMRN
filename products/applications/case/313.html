Temperature Indication Alarm for Molding Equipment

Case Number 313

last update: July 7, 2009

This application displays and monitors the temperature of molding equipment.

A Temperature Sensor detects the temperature status of the molding equipment. The application monitors the temperature with two selectable display colors (green and red) and a Temperature Indicator with a position meter. The system outputs an alarm when a temperature abnormality occurs.

Know-How

The system uses two selectable display colors (green and red) and a Temperature Indicator with a position meter that makes it easy to intuitively see the status using a bar chart.

Benefit / Efficiency

You can see the temperature status of molding equipment at a glance.
