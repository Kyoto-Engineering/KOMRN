Detects Black Tires at Long Distance

Case Number 388

last update: March 7, 2011

These sensors are minimally affected by backgrounds, enabling them to accurately detect black objects, such as tires, at a distance.

Know-How

Black rubber having low reflection factor can be detected only at a short distance, but the detection at the long distance is now possible with this sensor.

Benefit / Efficiency

Detection at a long distance can reduce the restrictions on installation.
