Monitoring Liquid Chemicals

Case Number 299

last update: July 7, 2009

This application displays the resistance of chemicals and other liquids.

Displaying the resistance of chemicals and other liquids that are used for semiconductors enables quality control for liquid chemicals.

Know-How

The resistance is displayed with a K3HB-X Process Indicator, which is ideal to measure and judge analog quantities.

Benefit / Efficiency

You can monitor liquid chemicals at a glance.
