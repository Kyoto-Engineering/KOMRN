Detects Passage of Candies or Cookies

Case Number 268

last update: April 13, 2015

This application uses an Area-sensing Fiber Sensor.
This Area-sensing Fiber Sensor eliminates the effect of the detected position of workpieces.

Know-How

It was very difficult to adjust the beam of conventional sensors to detect workpieces on conveyers.

Benefit / Efficiency

You can detect the presence of workpieces on conveyers if you install the Sensor so that part of 30-mm-wide line beam is on the conveyer.
