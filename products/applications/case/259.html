Ionizer Removes Electrostatic Charge from Plastic Parts

Case Number 259

last update: April 18, 2011

The air-blow ionizer removes electrostatic charge from preforms and blows off adhered dust.
The air-blow ionizer removes electrostatic charge from preforms before they become PET bottles, and blows off adhered dust.

Know-How

A high-speed valve and regulator together supply air used to reliably remove dust with ‘pulse air.’

Benefit / Efficiency

This prevents quality problems caused by dust.
