Low-cost Position-proportional Control for Brewing Equipment

Case Number 336

last update: December 7, 2009

You can use the E5EN-H Temperature Controller with position-proportional control to achieve low-cost position-proportional control.

Control of control valves is possible with the E5EN-H Temperature Controller with position-proportional control. This enables a low-cost position-proportional control system.
