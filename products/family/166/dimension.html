Applicable Thermistors

Use Element Interchangeable Thermistors (E52-THE5A, E52-THE6D, and E52-THE6F) to connect to the E5CS-[]GU. For details on Sensors, refer to E52.

Hard Protective Cover

The Y92A-48B Hard Protective Cover is available for the following applications.
• To protect the set from dust and dirt.
• To prevent the panel from being accidentally touched causing displacement of set values.
• To provide effective protection against water droplets.
