Repeater Unit application
conditions		 Up to 64 Repeater Units can be connected per network (i.e., per Master Unit). Up to 32
Repeater Units can be connected per trunk line or per sub-trunk line. When Repeater
Units are connected in series from the Master Unit, up to two extra segment layers can
be created (i.e., up to 2 Repeater Units are allowed between a Slave Unit and the Master
Unit).
Signal lines Two lines: BDH (communications data high) and BDL (communications data low)
Power lines Two lines: BS+ and BS- (power for communications and internal Slave Unit circuits)
