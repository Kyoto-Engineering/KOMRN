H8GN

The product cannot be made waterproof when gang-mounted.

  • Insert the H8GN in the square cutout, insert the adapter from the back, and push the H8GN into the cutout as far as possible. Use screws to secure the H8GN.
    To make the H8GN waterproof, insert waterproof packing and tighten the screws.
  • When mounting two or more products in a cutout, be sure that the ambient temperature does not exceed the specifications.
  • The mounting panel thickness must be 1 to 5 mm.
