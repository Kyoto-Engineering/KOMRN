Mounting

Use M4 screws with plane washers and spring washers to mount the Switch. Tighten each mounting screw securely to a torque of 1.18 to 1.47 Nm.

When mounting the Switch to a panel, use a tightening torque of 2.94 to 4.9 Nm for the hexagonal nuts on the actuator.
