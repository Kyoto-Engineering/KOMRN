Bar Light
FLV-BR Series

  • Ideal for illumination of wide, rectangular surfaces.
  • Many color and size variations.

Direct Ring Light
FLV-DR Series

  • Bright illumination with high-density LED arrays.
  • Compact designs that save installation space.
  • Optional Diffusion Plates for uniform illumination.

Low Angle Ring Light
FLV-DL Series

  • Bright illumination with high-density LED arrays.
  • Compact designs that save installation space.
  • Optional Diffusion Plates for uniform illumination.

Coaxial Light
FLV-CL Series

  • Long life and stability result from a structure with optimum heat dissipation.
  • Uniform illumination for clear images.

Shadowless Light
FLV-FR Series

  • Special diffusion plates create greater uniformity in lighting than normal ring lighting.

Shadowless Light
FLV-FP Series

  • Shadowless Ring Lighting
  • Achieve highly uniform illumination and obtain different images at different installation distances for a much wider range of application compared to normal ring lighting.

Shadowless Light
FLV-FS Series

  • Uniquely designed diffusion plate achieve highly uniform illumination through reflection and diffusion.
  • Eliminates the influences of small surface irregularities to highlight features through large differences in inclination.
  • Saves space for small workpieces while achieving the benefits of dome lighting.

Shadowless Light
FLV-FQ Series

  • Shadowless Square Lighting
  • Achieves highly uniform illumination and obtains different images at different installation distances for a much wider range of applications compared to normal ring lighting.

Spot Light
FLV-EP50 Series

  • Superior directional characteristic, essentially parallel light, and long-distance illumination.

High-power Spot Light
FLV-EP08 Series

  • High-power LEDs generate strong light with a compact design.
  • Ideal for applications in combination with a Coaxial Lens.
  • Highly efficient heat-dissipating structure ensures a long life.

Direct Back Light
FLV-DB Series

  • Highly uniform backlighting with high-density LED arrays. Emphasizes the outline features of workpieces.

Edge Type Light
FLV-FB Series

  • Five size variations with emitting surfaces from 35 mm square to 164 mm square.
  • As thin as 8 mm (FLV-FB7070).

Edge Type Coaxial Light
FLV-FX Series

  • High uniformity with diffused illumination.
  • Achieves both shadowless and coaxial illumination.
  • Lightweight and compact to conveniently fit into narrow spaces.

Dome Light
FLV-DD Series

  • Achieves uniform illumination by reflecting light from a ring-shaped light source through a highly reflective, diffusion dome.

Line Light
FLV-LN Series

  • Extremely high brightness
  • Achieves highly effective line illumination with a condenser lens.

Camera-mount Lighting Controller for FLV Series
FLV-TCC Series

  • Saves space with a compact design.
    No need for space in control panels for expansion.
  • Maintain Lighting intensity even with long wiring distances.
  • No complicated wiring from the Vision System Controller.

Analog Lighting Controller for FLV Series
FLV-ATC Series

  • Stationary type suitable for separate installation when no space near the Camera.
  • Light emission trigger can be input directly even without Vision Sensor.
