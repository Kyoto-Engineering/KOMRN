Head and Lever Positions

  • The operating head can be positioned and locked in any of four 90° positions and a lever can lock in any position through 360° around the shaft of the Limit Switch. Furthermore, the lever can be reversed and attached to the shaft (refer to the figures below on the right hand side). Therefore the roller is compatible with a wide movement range of a dog.
  • A Fork Lever Lock can be used with maintained models (D4A-0005N) only.
