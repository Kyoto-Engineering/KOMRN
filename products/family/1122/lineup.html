Ordering Individually

Displays, Lamps, and Sockets can be ordered separately. Combinations that are not available as sets can be created using individual Units. Also, store the parts as spares for maintenance and repairs.

* The Socket is compatible with LED-lighted, LED lamp-lighted, and incandescent lamp-lighted models.
