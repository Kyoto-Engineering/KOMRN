Socket Ratings

Features

  • Easily mounts or dismounts the G7T I/O Relay.
  • Also mounts the Indicator Module (with surge suppressing function).
  • Only 19 mm in width.
  • Terminals corresponding to the NO and NC contacts of a Relay are arranged on top of the Socket to enhance maintainability.
  • Also permits mounting of the G3TA Solid-state I/O Relay.
