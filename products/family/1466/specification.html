Item Value Definition
Reliability (MTBF) 135,000 hrs min. MTBF stands for Mean Time Between Failures, which is calculated according
to the probability of accidental device failures, and indicates reliability of devices.
Therefore, it does not necessarily represent a life of the product.
Life expectancy 10 yrs. min. The life expectancy indicates average operating hours under the ambient
temperature of 40°C and a load rate of 50%.
Normally this is determined by the life expectancy of the built-in aluminum
