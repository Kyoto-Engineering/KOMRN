General Specifications

Communications power supply voltage 11 to 25 VDC (Supplied from the communications connector.)
Communications power supply current
consumption 		DRT2-ID32B(V)(-1) : 100 mA
DRT2-OD32B(V)(-1) : 120 mA
DRT2-MD32B(V)(-1) : 110 mA
Noise immunity Conforms to IEC61000-4-4, 2 kV (power line)
Vibration resistance 10 to 60 Hz, 0.7-mm double amplitude, 60 to 150 Hz, 50 m/s2 for 80 min each in the X, Y,
and Z directions
Shock resistance 150m/s2, 6 directions, 3 times each
Dielectric strength 500 VAC (between isolated circuits)
Insulation resistance 20 M Ω min. (between isolated circuits)
Ambient operating temperature -10°C to 55°C
Ambient operating humidity 25% to 85% (with no condensation)
Ambient operating atmosphere No corrosive gases
Ambient storage temperature -25°C to 65°C
Mounting method M4 screw mounting
Weight 50 g max.
