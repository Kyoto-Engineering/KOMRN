       voltage to exceed the voltage range. When adjusting the output voltage, confirm the actual output voltage from the

*8. The range for compliance with EC Directives and safety standards (UL, EN, etc.) is 100 to 240 VAC (85 to 264 VAC).
*9. The noise level depends on the wiring method and other factors. Insert one clamp filter (the ZCAT2436-1330A from
       TDK) as a noise countermeasure on the input line and ground line combined.
