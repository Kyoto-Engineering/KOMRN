Applicable Cables

Select one of the specified Connector Plugs from the following table.

XS2F-D421-[]80-F (For DC)
XS2F-A421-[]90-F (For AC)

Special Mounting Plates (Limit Switches are not attached to the Plates.)

D4C-P001 (For D4CC-[]001)

D4C-P002 (For D4CC-[]002)

D4C-P020 (For D4CC-[]024)

Note: Unless otherwise specified, a tolerance of ±0.4 mm applies to all dimensions.

Remarks

There is no difference in mounting pitch between the Mounting Plate and the WL. The mounting depth of the D4CC with the Mounting Plate attached is, however, shorter than that of the panel-mounted WL.
