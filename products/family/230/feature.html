H8BM-R

Multi-maintenance Counter/Timer (DIN 72 x 72)

Nine Built-in Counters/Timers to Measure Equipment Operating Cycles and Times and Forecast Maintenance Timing

Provides up to nine counters or accumulative timers. (Counter and timer functions can be used at the same time.)

Individual forecast outputs to indicate maintenance timing.

Pre-forecast display and machine stoppage output provided.

IP54 oil-proof type at setting area for resistance to oil and water.

Separate digit keys to easily change settings.

Compact, short-body: 72 x 72 x 79 mm (DIN).

Key protection function prevents incorrect operation.

Multiple outputs: NPN/PNP.

Directly connectable to 2-wire DC sensors.

Complies with UL and CSA.
