Note: Silicon rubber is used to increase resistance to the environment. Silicon rubber, however, can generate silicon gas.
          (This can occur at room temperature, but the amount of silicon gas generated increases at higher temperatures.)
          Silicon gas will react as a result of arc energy and form silicon oxide (SiO2). If silicon oxide accumulates on the
          contacts, contact interference can occur and can interfere with the device. Before using a Switch, test it under actual
          application conditions (including the environment and operating frequency) to confirm that no problems will occur in
          actual.
