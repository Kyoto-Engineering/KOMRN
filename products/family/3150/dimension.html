Sheathed Platinum Resistance Thermometers

Exposed-lead Models

E52-P[]AY

Lead Wire
• Standard (-20°C to 70°C): Fully vinyl-covered with twelve 0.18-dia conductors (0.3 mm thick) and 4.8 mm in outer dia.
  The sleeve resists a temperature range between 0°C and 70°C.
• Heat Resistive (0°C to 180°C): Fully glass-wool-covered with thirty 0.12-dia. conductors (0.3 mm thick) externally
  shielded with stainless steel, 4 mm in outer dia. The sleeve resists a temperature range between 0°C and 100°C.
• Lead Wire Length (M): 1, 2, 4, or 8 m

Enclosed-terminal Models
