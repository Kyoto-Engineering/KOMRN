Illumination Only and Colored Illumination for Models with LEDs

With illumination only, the color of the lighted surface is the same when the LED is lit and when it is not lit.
Example: Red Illumination

With colored illumination, the color of the lighted surface is white when the LED is not lit and the LED emits another color when it is lit.
Example: Red Illumination

Ordering: For colored illumination, order the Pushbutton, Case, Lamp, and Switch separately.
