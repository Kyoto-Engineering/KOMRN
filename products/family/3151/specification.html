          3. The thermocouple is not of airtight construction.

Installation Example

Cut a thread into the workpiece, and screw in the thermocouple while pushing in so that the tip makes complete contact.

Note: E52-CA1DY with the same shape and multiple element wires are also available (E52-CA1DY-40).
