IO-Link Master Unit

Photoelectric Sensors

Photoelectric Sensors detect photo-optical workpieces. Models are available with standard models (diffuse-reflective, through-beam, retro-reflective) and color mark sensor.

Proximity Sensors

Proximity Sensors are available in models using high-frequency oscillation to detect ferrous and non-ferrous metal objects. Models are available with standard models and spatter-resistant models.
