Push Buttons / Indicator Lamps

Push Button Switches are manually operated switches that are available in many different types: Switches with round or square bodies for mounting in round or square holes, lighted and non-lighted Switches, Indicators, Selector Switches, and more.

φ16 Type

These Pushbutton Switches mount in holes with a 16-mm diameter and are available with or without lights. They come with a short body of only 28.5 mm. Both Knob-type and Key-type Selector Switches as well as Buzzers and Indicators are available.

φ22(φ25) Type

These Pushbutton Switches mount in holes with a 22.3-mm diameter. The Switch Unit and Operation Unit are connected and disconnected using a lever. Both Knob-type and Key-type Selector Switches as well as Indicators are available.

φ30 Type

These push button switch can be mounted to 30mm diameter panel hole and can use the contact block, mounting collar and LED lamp of A22N series as common parts.

Non-lighted Pushbutton Switch

These Non-lighted Pushbutton Switches mount in holes with a 10.5-mm diameter. A built-in basic switch provides a sure click feel in operation. The Operation Unit is available in six colors: Blue, black, green, red, white, and yellow.

Square / Rectangular-body Type

OMRON also provides these Square Body Pushbutton Switches and Indicators. They are available in many different sizes. Either total surface lighting or partial lighting is possible.

Plateau Head

Plateau-head with Round-body Type Theses flat top Pushbutton Switches are newly introduced. The Switch Unit can be detached from Operation Unit.
