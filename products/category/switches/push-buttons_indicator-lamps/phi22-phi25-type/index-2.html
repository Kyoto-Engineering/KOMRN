φ22(φ25) Type

These Pushbutton Switches mount in holes with a 22.3-mm diameter. The Switch Unit and Operation Unit are connected and disconnected using a lever. Both Knob-type and Key-type Selector Switches as well as Indicators are available.

φ22(φ25) Type List

There are 8 products of φ22(φ25) Type.

Key-type Selector Switches A22NK

22-mm dia. Key-type Selector Switches. Control panel miniaturization through a more compact design and modified wiring direction. Addition of Push-In Plus terminal blocks for easy wiring.

Pushbutton Switches A22NN / A22NL

22-mm dia. Pushbutton Switches. Control panel miniaturization through a more compact design and modified wiring direction. Addition of Push-In Plus terminal blocks for easy wiring.

Selector Switches A22NS / A22NW

22-mm dia. Knob-type Selector Switches. Control panel miniaturization through a more compact design and modified wiring direction. Addition of Push-In Plus terminal blocks for easy wiring.

Indicators M22N

22-mm dia. Indicators. Control panel miniaturization through a more compact design and modified wiring direction. Addition of Push-In Plus terminal blocks for easy wiring.
