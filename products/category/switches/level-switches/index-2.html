Level Switches

These devices equip electrodes to detect liquid levels. They have been widely used in water works and sewers for buildings and housing complexes, industrial facilities and equipment, water treatment plants and sewage treatment facilities, and many other applications.

Floatless Level Controllers

The 61F model can be selected according to the control method, installation method, liquid to be detected, wiring length, and other requirements.

Electrode Holders / Electrodes

Electrode Holders and Electrodes can be selected according to the inside of a tank, environment around the tank facilities, and control range.

Water Leak Detectors

Stable detection of leaks can be achieved. The model can be selected according to the output form, installation method, and other requirements.
