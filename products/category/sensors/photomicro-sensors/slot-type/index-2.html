Slot-type

The emitter and receiver are set in a U shape to enable easy handling.

There are 7 products of Slot-type.

Ultra-compact Pre-wired Photomicrosensor (Non-modulated) EE-SX95

Meeting Customer Needs with Ultracompact Sensors that Mount with M3 Screws

Broad Slot-type Photomicrosensor EE-SPX303N / SPX403N

A Wide Slot Width of 13 mm and Superior Resistance to Light Interference and Noise.

Photomicrosensor with Slim Cable (Non-modulated) EE-SX77 / SX87

Slim, Compact Photomicrosensor that is still easy to use.

Slot-type Photomicrosensor with connector or pre-wired models (Non-modulated) EE-SX47 / SX67

Global Standard Slot-type photomicrosensors with 50- to 100-mA direct switching capacity.

Slot-type Photomicrosensor EE-SX97

Built-in connector enables downsizing and easier connection. Protective circuit for safe operation.

Slot-type Photomicrosensor with Connector (Modulated) EE-SPX74 / SPX84
