Temperature Controllers

These Controllers receive sensor signals and control heaters or other devices to maintain a preset temperature. They can also be used for humidity, pressure, and flowrate control. OMRON also provides temperature and humidity sensors.

General-purpose

OMRON General-purpose Controllers meet a wide range of applications needs for food processing machines, packaging machines, extruders, semiconductor production equipment, and many other areas with high speed, high performance, easy settings, and easy-to-read desplays.

Economy Type

These are relatively simple in functions and economic Temperature Controllers. They can also be used for temperature alarm application.

Module Type

Module Type

PLC Unit Type

Temperature control unit of PLC. At most 4 points of temperature control is possible by 1 unit.

Support Software

The Support Software is an application software for Temperature Controller to set its parameters and monitor temperature.

Input Equipments

Temperature Sensors and Temperature/Humidity Sensors for Temperature Controllers are available for various applications.

Related Equipments

USB-Serial Conversion Cable is also available to connect to Temperature Controllers.
