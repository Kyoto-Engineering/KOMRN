Heater burnout detection

There are Detectors for High-precision Detection of heater burnout , High-capacity (4 to 80 A AC) Detectors with built-in CTs, and Compact Plug-in Burnout Detectors with low capacities (0.5 to 4 A AC).

Heater burnout detection List

There are 2 products of Heater burnout detection.

Digital Heater Element Burnout Detector K8AC-H

A high-precision Heater Element Burnout Detector compatible with a wide-range of heater control methods.

Heater Element Burnout Detector K2CU

Accurate Detection of Heater Element Burnout Regardless of Heater Capacities
