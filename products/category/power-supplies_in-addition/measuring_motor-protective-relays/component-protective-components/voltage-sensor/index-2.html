Voltage detection

Voltage Detectors are available for detecting undervoltage and overvoltages for AC or DC power. AC and DC Voltage Detection Relays that can be operated and reset are also available.

Voltage detection List

There are 2 products of Voltage detection.

Voltage Sensor SDV

Overvoltage/Undervoltage Monitoring Relay for AC and DC Input

Voltage Sensor LG2

Compact, Economical Plug-in Type Voltage Sensing Relay
