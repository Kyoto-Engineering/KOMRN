General Purpose Relays

OMRON provides General-purpose Relays, I/O Relays, Power Relays, Latching Relays, and Ratchet Relays.

For Control Panel

A wide range of products are available for the purpose of sequence control to direct load switching, which will suit for various applications depending on the control method or environment inside of a panel.

For Built-in

Multi-pole Relays provide high capacity and high dielectric strength. The G7L and G7J can be used selectively according to the characteristics of a compressor, a heater, or a motor.

Special-purpose

Special Relay can be used for alternating operation of motor drives.
