Safety Switches

Switches for persons who operate machines manually in the hazard zone, when robot teaching, process changing and performing maintenance.

Safety Switches List

There are 4 products of Safety Switches.

Guard Lock Safety Key Selector Switch A22LK

The guard lock prevents accidental mode changes.

Safety Key Selector Switch A22TK

Key-type Selector Switch with Direct Opening Mechanism

Enabling Grip Switch A4EG

Enabling grip switches are safety components used to prevent unexpected machine operation when workers perform maintenance or other non-routine tasks inside hazardous areas, such as areas inside protective shields.

Enabling Switch A4E

3-position Enabling Switch for Safer Robot Operation
