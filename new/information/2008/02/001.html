Much more detailed product information pages released

February 6, 2008

OMRON IA now provides much more detailed 
Product pages

You can access much more detailed information about the main items in four categories - Photoelectric Sensors, Proximity Sensors, Rotary Encoders and Power Supplies.
Look for other categories to be added soon, including Relays, Timers, Limit Switches etc.

Four Product Categories with detailed information

Omron by Kyoto engineering & Automation Website now provides detailed product pages in following four categories.
With this new function, you can select from a general category, move to a specific product group, and quickly find the detail specifications of each Model Number you need.

Sensors

  • Photoelectric Sensors
  • Proximity Sensors
  • Rotary Encoders

Power Supplies & In Addition

  • Power Supplies

How to access to the Model Number information

  1. For example, on the Photoelectric Sensor E3Z page, 
    you can go to the new “Item Index” tab, 
    and easily select the desired Model Number.
  2. Use our newly created tabs, including Ratings/Performance, 
    Timing Chart, Operating Range etc. , to quickly access
    the detailed information you need.
