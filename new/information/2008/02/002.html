Recommended products infromation added on FAQ and Technical Guide

February 8, 2008

You can move directly to Recommended Products 
from “FAQ” and “Technical Guide”

OMRON IA now provides a convenient function, by which you can move directly to Recommended Products from "FAQ" and "Technical Guide" pages. Also, on each "FAQ" page, you can find related FAQ. With this function you can gain a more complete understanding of OMRON IA Products and your question.

  • Technical Guide
  • FAQ

From "Technical Guide"

On the lower part for each “Technical Guide” page, 
you can find Recommended Products, and move to 
the products page with just one click.

e.g. Case of Technical Guide for Photomicro Sensors

From "FAQ"

On the lower part for each “FAQ” page, you can find 
Recommended Products and also other related FAQ.

e.g. Case of FAQ03435 of Vision Sensors FAQ
