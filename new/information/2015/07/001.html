Omron IA Japan is the best BtoB website of the year 2015 for nine consecutive years.

July 1, 2015

Japanese website; OMRON IA Japan has been recognized as the best BtoB website of the year 2015 for nine consecutive years in Japan, according to the research done by Japan Brand Strategy, Inc. (JBS), Tokyo, Japan. Omron was evaluated especially on the access experience and degree of sufficiency for demand.
With this honor as encouragement, all of us at Omron will continue to do our best to improve our website and the quality of service and support for our customers.

Investigation Summary

The following subjects have been investigated by Japan Brand Strategy, Inc.
Object: 240 sites in Japanese that introduce products/ services for business between companies (B2B).
Period: 23/04/2015 - 07/05/2015
Method: Questionnaires from 10,000 persons
