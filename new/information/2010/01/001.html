New Year's Greetings

January 1, 2010

Please accept our hearty thanks for visiting the “Omron by Kyoto engineering & Automation Global” website, and our New Year greetings.

By matching market demand for quality, safety and environment with our sensing and control technology, we aim to realize innovation in “monozukuri.”
