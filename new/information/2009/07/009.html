OMRON IA Japan has been ranked first among B2B websites in Japan for 2009

July 24, 2009

Japanese website OMRON IA Japan has been ranked first among B2B websites in Japan for 2009 by Japan Brand Strategy, Inc.

First place in the general evaluation category for three consecutive years.

OMRON IA Japan is the best B2B website in Japan in 2009, according to research done by Japan Brand Strategy, Inc., Tokyo, Japan. OMRON IA Japan has been ranked first in the general evaluation category for three consecutive years by JBS,Inc. "The access experience on the job," and "degree of sufficiency for demand"are important evaluation points.
OMRON IA Japan was given the highest evaluation on both points.
With this honor as encouragement, all of us at OMRON will continue to do our best to improve our website.
We thank you for visiting our website.

Investigation summary
Object : 252 sites in Japanese that introduce
products / services for business between
companies (B2B.)
Method : Questionnaires from 10,387 persons.
