OMRON exhibits SEMICON Japan 2007

December 10, 2007

OMRON will exhibit at "SEMICON Japan 2007," to be held December 5-7 in Makuhari, Japan. It is one of the most important exhibitions in the semiconductor industry.
OMRON will exhibit various new products, including sensing components and temperature controllers and so on. We hope to see you there!

[Note] (as of December 10, 2007) :
  • This event has already been finished. Thanks to many customers to vist our booth.
