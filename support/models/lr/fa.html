Machine Automation Controllers

Model Ratings Environmental Category Remarks
NJ --- ENV1,2,3 *
NX --- ENV2,3 *
* General Power distribution Zones and/or Bridge Zone
Note 1: The above table lists series names. Ask your OMRON representative for details on specific model numbers.
Note 2: When using as an LR product, attach a noise filter on the power supply line.
Use the TAH-06-683 from Cosel Co., Ltd., for the noise filter.
Note 3: NJ-series and NX-series PLC are certified for the bridge. For type approvals, these models shall not be installed on the deck or on machinery that has severe vibration conditions.

ENV5 Open decks - 　-25°C to +70°C
Note: Models that are certified for an ENV2 environment can also be used in an ENV1 environment.
