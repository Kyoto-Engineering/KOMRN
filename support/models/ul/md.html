Note 1: The above table lists series names. Ask your OMRON representative for details on specific model numbers.
Note 2: Products that are certified for ANSI/ISA 12.12.01 can be used in hazardous locations for Class I, Div. 2, Groups A, B, C, and D.
Description of Hazardous Locations:
[Class]
Class I: Locations in which flammable gases or vapors may be present in the air in sufficient quantities to be explosive or ignitable.
Class II: Locations in which there is combustible dust.
Class III: Locations in which easily ignitable fibers or flyings are present
but are not airborne in sufficient quantities to ignite.
[Division]
Division 1: Locations in which explosive or ignitable material is normally present or is present at certain times.
Division 2: Locations in which explosive or ignitable material is not normally present (but may accidentally exist).
【Group】
Class I :
Group A: Acetylene
Group B: Combustible gas/vapor, example: hydrogen
Group C: Combustible gas/vapor, example: ethylene
Group D: Combustible gas/vapor, example: propane
