Note 1: The above table lists series names. Ask your OMRON representative for details on specific model numbers.
Note 2: NS-series PTs are certified for the bridge.
　　　When using an NS-series PT on the bridge, attach a noise filter and an electromagnetic shield.
　　　Use the TAH-06-683 from Cosel Co., Ltd., for the noise filter.
　　　Use the E09F-100 or E09R13502P from Seiwa Electric Mfg. Co., Ltd., for the electromagnetic shield.
　　　Refer to the product manuals for details.
Note 3: For type approvals, these models shall not be installed on the deck, in the bridge, or on machinery that has severe vibration conditions.
　　　NS and NA5-series PTs can be installed on the bridge.
Note 4: For use in the bridge, additional power supply filter Schaffner FN-2080-xx is to be connected to the NA5-series power terminals.
Type approval of the NK (Nippon Kaiji Kyokai) was made according to Chapter 1, Part 7 of Guidance for the Approval and Type Approval of Materials and Equipment for Marine Use.
