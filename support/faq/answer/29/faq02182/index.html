Question

What causes contact welding and what can be done about it?

Answer

ï»¿

Causes:

Overload that does not match the contact switching capacity

Inrush current larger than rating

Breaking current larger than rating

Switching frequency in excess of allowable operating frequency

Usage in a location subject to continuous vibration

Countermeasures:

Switch the load using a relay or a contactor.

A protective circuit is required so that loads such as relays, motors, incandescent bulbs, and solenoids can be used properly.
