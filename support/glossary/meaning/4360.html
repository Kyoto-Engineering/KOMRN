compensating leadwire

Definition

The appropriate product category
  • Temperature Controllers
An insulated pair of conductor with essentially the same thermoelectric characteristics as the thermocouple is used together with over a wide temperature range, including room temperature. The thermocouple terminal is connected to the reference junction to compensate for error caused by the temperature of the thermocouple terminal section.
