special reduced torque characteristics

Definition

The appropriate product category
  • Inverters
Characteristics that are virtually constant with the square of the speed (i.e., characteristics at which the torque generation curve is a square curve) and do not require a large torque at low speeds. Examples: Loads such as fans and pumps.
